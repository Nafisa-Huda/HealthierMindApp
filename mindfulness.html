What Is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the ability to pay full attention to any task you are doing. This can be playing a video game, studying, or even as simple as eating a meal! Being mindful means you are taking things slow to really focus and enjoy what you are doing. This is the opposite of rushing or multitasking, rather you are taking your time, staying focused and relaxing your way into the task at hand.

So basically if you can develop the ability to be mindful in your day to day tasks, you are building your own superpower. Being mindful can help you in just about every part of life. By practicing and training your mind from a young age, you can learn to control your emotions and focus your attention on just about anything. Like any sport, pracice makes perfect and if you continue to practice mindfulness exercises, you will develop a natural instinct to being mindful in everything you do.
